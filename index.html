Meet Dr Fathima M, (PT)

Dr Fathima received her Bachelor in Physiotherapy from the Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences,Karnataka.Dr Fathima is also a life time member of Kerala Association for Physiotherapists Coordination (KAPC).

Dr Fathima has clinical experience in the outpatient orthopedic setting, where she treated patients with various conditions .Dr Fathima’s clinical background allowed her to gain exposure to different aspects of the physical therapy profession, which she uses to provide the best patient care possible.Dr Fathima also believes in empowering her patients through an educational approach so they can take control of their rehabilitation.

Our Services

Physiotherapy

Our Cochin Physiocare is proud to offer state-of-the-art, compassionate care. By sharing knowledge and investing in research, Cochin Physiocare empowers our therapists in advancing the field and improving patient quality of life.

Rehabilitation

Our care is team-based and “whole person” focused. Our continuing mission is to help each and every patient achieve sobriety and wellness in body, mind, and spirit. Recovery is possible with the right tools.

Mobile Services

Providing service as per your convenience ,Cochin Physiocare is also a mobile physiotherapy service which allows patients to be seen in the comfort of their own home, care home or office.
